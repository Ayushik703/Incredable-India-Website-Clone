India is blessed with heritage riches and a regal lineage. Vestiges of Indian royalty can be felt in the various forts and palaces speckled across the country. From hill forts in Rajasthan to formidable fortifications in Gwalior, Golconda and Delhi, the history of India stands tall and proud, housed inside these marvellous structures. To experience how the royalty lived in the past, several of these palace-turned heritage hotels are a must-visit.


Visiting these forts and palaces is the best way to uncover a real treasure of Indian history. These serve as great reminders of the bygone eras, mystical kingdoms and their rulers.
